Webtoon Platform FAQ

A webtoon is a digital comic that is typically read by scrolling vertically. It originated in South Korea and has become popular worldwide due to its easy accessibility and engaging format.

Many webtoons are available for free, but some may have premium episodes or features that require payment. Check each webtoon’s details for specific information.

Yes, you can add your favorite webtoons to a “Favorites” or “Library” section so you can easily access them later. Just click on the heart or bookmark icon on the webtoon’s page.

Some platforms allow users to download webtoons for offline reading, but this feature may be limited to premium users or specific webtoons. Check the app or website for more details.

You can support creators by purchasing episodes, using in-app currency to unlock premium content, or sharing their work with others. Some platforms may also offer tipping features.
